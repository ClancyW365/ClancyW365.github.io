About Us

Foundation

ABC Technical Training Institute is a learning institution that was chartered by the government of Kenya last year in September and the learning commences this May after the April intake. This institude was a solution to cab the increased rate of students leaving high school.

Mission

We are dedicated to naturing a learning environment that will enable all students to prosper on their field of choice. Competence is a very important factor that we aim to equip all students for we believe that, Competence makes a man confident

Vision

We aim to be among the most Trusted technical training institute in the Republic offering the most courses that represents all. We are focused to ever increase the Quality of our training to ensure naturing of confidence through guaranteed competence.


Our Motto: Strive for excellence.


What we offer

We offer courses ranging from a variety of subject of interest including:

  • School of Pure, Applied and Health Science.
  • School of Hospitality and Tourism.
  • School of Computing and Information Technology
  • School of Education, Humanities and Social Science
